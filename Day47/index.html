• Radicals are building blocks
    of Chinese characters

    For example, radical 木(tree) is used as building blocks for a lot of characters. Double 木 forms 林(woods), while triple 木 becomes 森(forest).

  • Most of the radicals
    resemble objects

    For example, radial 山 means mountain and its pictographic form depicts mountain peaks.
